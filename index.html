About Cryptocurrency Markets

Cryptocurrency markets operate 24 hours a day, 7 days a week unlike traditional stock markets. Bitcoin (BTC) is the world's first and largest cryptocurrency by market capitalization. Ethereum (ETH) is the second largest and powers thousands of decentralized applications.

Crypto prices are highly volatile and can change rapidly. This makes cryptocurrency trading both exciting and risky. Use NovaPulse to practice and build your trading skills safely before investing real money.

What is Bitcoin?

Bitcoin (BTC) was created in 2009 by an anonymous person known as Satoshi Nakamoto. It is a decentralized digital currency that operates without a central bank or government. Bitcoin transactions are verified by a network of computers and recorded on a public ledger called the blockchain.

What is Ethereum?

Ethereum (ETH) is a decentralized platform launched in 2015 that enables developers to build and deploy smart contracts and decentralized applications. Ether is the native cryptocurrency of the Ethereum network and is used to pay for transactions and computational services.
